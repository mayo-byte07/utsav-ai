---

🏗️ Project Structure
The platform is built to provide a seamless interface between you and our management team:

🖥️ Client Portal (/utsavai-frontend)
Framework: React 18 (TypeScript) + Vite

Interface: Clean, minimal dashboard for clients to view event status, approve designs, and chat with their dedicated coordinator.

Styling: Tailwind CSS + Framer Motion for a premium, high-end feel.

Tracking: Interactive Gantt charts and budget trackers.

⚙️ Management Engine (/utsavai-backend)
Framework: Node.js + Express (TypeScript)

Database: MongoDB for secure storage of event blueprints and vendor contracts.

Communication: Socket.io for real-time updates and instant messaging with our support team.

Security: Full JWT-based authentication for data privacy.

🚀 How It Works
1.Submit Your Vision: Use the platform to input your event type, guest count, and preferences.

2.We Take Over: Our team assigns a lead coordinator who scouts locations and secures the best vendors.

3.Approve & Relax: Use your dashboard to approve vendor selections and view the evolving mood board.

4.Execution: We manage the on-site logistics, ensuring everything runs perfectly on the day of the event.
